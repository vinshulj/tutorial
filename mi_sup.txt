linear regration:- 
-> small-midium size data,low-medium dimentionality ,linear,regretion
asumption:- linearity,independence of error ,homoscedasticity,no multicolinearity
pro. fast, easy to impliment ,low computational cost
con. prone to overfitting for high dimention data, can not handle outlier,sparse data 

logistic regration:-
-> small to large data size, linear , sparse and high dimention,classification
assumption:- independent feature,binary dependent,large sample size
pro: work well with sparse data,very fast,probabilistic model
cons. poor with non-linearity problem
